Didi Pizza - Fresh pizza daily in the heart

Are you looking for a great stop in the heart of Hoi An to enjoy the true, fresh pizza every day? Do not miss Didi Pizza, a small but impressive small pizza shop, which brings a different and memorable pizza experience.

Outstanding advantages:

  • Pizza refreshes every day - Thin, crispy powder, ingredients are always carefully selected and fresh.
  • Delicate flavor, unique - attractive topping, balanced flavor, light and easy to addicted.
  • Cozy, friendly space - Clean shop, gentle decoration, somewhat overlooking the street for you to enjoy the pace of the Hoi An Life
  • Dedicated service, fast - Customers praise attentive service, fast delivery, reasonable price.
  • Interesting interactive experience - You can see chef Didi performing bakery techniques, throwing pizza in front of guests

The dishes to try:

  • Gentle pizza can be enough for two people, suitable for light evening.
  • "Best Sellers" such as Catch of the Day, 4 Seasons, or traditional types such as Margarita, BBQ Chicken, Diavola.
